# Ecommerce-sales-dashboard-using-powerbi
Overview:

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of ecommerce sales performance, helping businesses monitor key metrics, identify profit trends, and analyze category-wise sales distribution.

Features: 

Sales Performance Tracking
Total Sales Amount: 438K
Total Profit: 37K
Total Orders/Quantity: 6K
Category Analysis
Visual breakdown of quantity sold across:
Clothing
Electronics
Furniture
Profit contribution comparison by product category.
Monthly Profit Trends
Interactive bar chart displaying profit performance across all months.
Quickly identifies high-performing and low-performing periods.
Quarter-wise Filtering
Dynamic quarter slicer (Q1–Q4) for focused analysis and reporting.

Dashboard Insights: 

Clothing contributes the highest share of sales quantity and profit.
Monthly profit trends reveal seasonal fluctuations, with peak profitability observed in November.
Certain months show negative profit, helping stakeholders identify periods requiring further investigation.

Business Value
